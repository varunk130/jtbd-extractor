JTBD Extractor

Turn raw research into Jobs-to-be-Done statements — reframe feature requests as underlying needs and uncover innovation opportunities.

How It Works

📥 Input

  • Interview transcripts
  • Survey responses
  • Support tickets
  • Customer feedback
  • Feature requests

⚡ JTBD Extractor Process

1 Review existing personas & product context
2 Identify what users are trying to accomplish
3 Format as When / I want to / So I can
4 Categorize into Functional · Emotional · Social
5 Score opportunities (importance × satisfaction gap)
6 Translate feature requests → underlying jobs

📊 Output

  • Structured JTBD statements
  • Opportunity score rankings
  • Feature → Job translation
  • Persona alignment map
  • Next-step recommendations
Three Dimensions of Every Job
⚙️

Functional Jobs

The practical task they need done
When preparing for a quarterly review,
I want to see my team's progress at a glance,
So I can present confidently without manual data gathering.
💜

Emotional Jobs

How they want to feel
When onboarding a new enterprise customer,
I want to feel confident and prepared,
So I can make a strong first impression without anxiety.
👥

Social Jobs

How they want to be perceived
When presenting AI strategy to leadership,
I want to be seen as innovative and data-driven,
So I can earn trust for larger budget asks.
Opportunity Scoring

Finding Where to Focus

Opportunity = Importance + (Importance − Satisfaction)
Higher scores reveal underserved jobs — high importance but low satisfaction. These are your biggest innovation opportunities.

Based on the Ulwick Outcome-Driven Innovation framework.

Feature Request Translation

From What They Said → To What They Need

The most powerful reframe: surface-level requests hide deeper jobs.
What They Asked ForThe Real Job
"Add a dashboard" → Know if I'm on track without manual checking
"Export to PDF" → Share progress with stakeholders who don't have access
"Slack integration" → Get notified about changes in my existing workflow
"More chart types" → Tell a compelling story with data to persuade leadership
"API access" → Connect this data to my other tools automatically
