Recommendations
1

Prioritize Auto-Generated Status Intelligence

#1 opportunity score and directly addresses the most emotionally charged pain point (executive anxiety). Quick win: AI-drafted weekly summaries from existing activity data.

2

Build the Dependency Impact Radar

Competitors have dependency tracking, but nobody does proactive cascade alerting well. Dynamic dependency graph with health signals = defensible moat.

3

Don't Build a Gantt Chart

The underlying job is dependency visibility, not the visualization. A dynamic dependency graph with health signals serves the real job better.

4

Surface Emotional Jobs in Marketing

"Sleep better knowing nothing's falling through the cracks" will resonate more than "Track tasks across teams." Lead with feelings, prove with features.
